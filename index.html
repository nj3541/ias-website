About IEEE IAS

The IEEE Industry Applications Society (IAS) is a global community of professionals dedicated to advancing the theory and practice of electrical and electronic engineering in industrial and commercial applications. IAS provides resources, technical knowledge, and networking opportunities for engineers, researchers, and students interested in industrial power, automation, energy systems, and emerging technologies.

Our mission is to foster innovation, share knowledge, and support the professional growth of our members through conferences, publications, and educational programs.
